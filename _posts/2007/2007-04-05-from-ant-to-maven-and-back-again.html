Just to remember:
Next time I think about switching the build process of a huge project from Ant to Maven, I will first read Zarar Siddiqi's blog and then never, ever have any doubts about my (awful but working) Ant scripts again. Never touch a running system, buster.

That said: I would never again start a project from scratch without Maven.
