--- layout: post title: Decrypting Jenkins Passwords date: '2013-07-02T13:18:00.000+02:00' author: martin tags: - CI - jenkins modified_time: '2013-07-02T13:18:23.915+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-987786588703208935.post-1374722903907684145 blogger_orig_url: http://bxm-dev.blogspot.com/2013/07/decrypting-jenkins-passwords.html --- A short hack to recover a password from the Jenkins configuration files:
  1. Retrieve the encrypted password from $JENKINS_HOME/config.xml
  2. Open the Jenkins Script Console
  3. Execute e.g.
    hudson.util.Secret.decrypt 'vceV2JWuTNIVc85PceFrk9C3u9AqB2nEQNg2a2xIA78='
